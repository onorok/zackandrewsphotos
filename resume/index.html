About Me

Hey there, i'm Zack Andrews. My love for computers began around age 14 when I built my first desktop. Ever since then I've been trying to figure out their inner workings and what makes them tick.

In highschool a friend and I began experimenting on GeoCities and Angelfire, building websites from scratch. We would challenge each other daily and see who could come up with the coolest design or functionality in the beginnings of CSS, Javascript, and HTML.

As far back as I can remember, I have been an artist. Of course, that love for art transposed over a love for computers pushed me towards a more design oriented development path and eventually led me to where I am today: a Front-end Developer.

I absolutely love the challenge of trying new things and overcoming obstacles in both code and design. I currently overcome those obstacles working as part of a great team at IBM.
